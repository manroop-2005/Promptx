# PromptX

Marketplace for AI **System and User prompts** – here you can find your desired prompts that give the exact output you need.

If you have excellent prompts, you can also sell them!

Integrated chatbot with system prompts for specific tasks like General AI for general tasks and CodingAI for coding tasks.

---

## Technology

1. **React Native**: Android and iOS app development.
2. **Cloudinary**: Image upload.
3. **Postgres with Prisma ORM**: Database.
4. **Express**: Backend.

---

## Features

1. **User Authentication**: JWT-based email and password sign-in and login.
2. **OAuth**: Sign in and login with Google.
3. **Integrated Chat Bot**: Chatbot with dedicated system prompts. Uses the `DeepSeel-R1` model for LLM, ensuring context is maintained during conversation.
4. **Marketplace**: Find prompts with search, filter, and category selection for easy discovery.
5. **Sell Your Prompt**: Sell your prompt! If you have a good prompt, users can pay and buy it.
6. **Subscription**: Subscription model with `Premium`, `Pro`, and a free tier.
7. **User Profile**: Profile with created, liked, purchased, recent liked, recent purchased prompts, delete chat history, and basic settings/help center features.
